# 🌸 ArovyaCare
### AI-Powered Maternal Health Risk Detection System

ArovyaCare is an intelligent healthcare platform designed to enable **early detection of pregnancy-related risks** in rural and underserved communities. It empowers **ASHA workers** with AI-driven insights to identify high-risk cases early and ensure timely intervention.

---

## 🚨 The Problem We're Solving

In many rural areas:

- ❌ Limited access to timely healthcare
- ❌ Early symptoms go unnoticed
- ❌ Hospitals are far (50–100 km away)
- ❌ ASHA workers manage multiple patients with limited tools

➡️ This leads to delayed diagnosis and increased risk for both mother and child.

---

## 💡 Our Approach

ArovyaCare provides a **simple, scalable digital solution**:

- 📱 Easy patient data entry
- 🤖 AI-based risk prediction using vitals and symptoms
- 📊 Real-time health insights
- 🚨 Early alerts for high-risk cases
- 🌐 Web-based platform accessible anywhere

👉 **Goal:** Early Detection → Timely Action → Better Outcomes

## 🧠 How It Works

1. ASHA worker enters patient details
2. System collects vitals and symptoms
3. AI model analyzes risk factors

### 📊 Output Generated

- Risk level (Low / Medium / High)
- Health insights
- Recommendations

🚨 High-risk cases are flagged for immediate action
